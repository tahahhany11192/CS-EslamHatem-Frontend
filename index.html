About Islam Hatem

Islam Hatem is a Computer Engineering student at the German University in Cairo (GUC), currently in his fourth year. He earned his IGCSE certificate from El Rowad International School (2019–2022), graduating with eight O-Levels, all graded between 8 and 9, and one AS-Level with grade A. He distinguished himself in Computer Science with a grade 9.

With five years of teaching experience, Islam has served as a teaching assistant under Eng. Amr Osama in eight sessions and has also provided two years of private tutoring. His teaching approach has consistently supported student success, with the majority achieving grades 8 and 9 (67%), followed by grade 7 (23%), and grade 6 or below (10%).
